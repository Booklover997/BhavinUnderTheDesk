## Funny Password Cracking Moments
I won't rant about the pw cracking hard challenge yet, but we did have some really funny moments near the end during the last three hours trying to crack our semi-uncrackable hash.

Specifically it was for the category called "Wyrmguard Bank". We thought we had a general map that it was something like theme -> something to do with that theme. 
```json
Dormitories -> Actual Dormitory Password 
Howler Hub -> Actual Person
Pensiever -> Potion Ingredient (Small magical basin) 
Spellbook -> Name of Spellbook
```
This led to us coming up with some pretty good, if a little wonky, guesses. Wyrm is used to indicate a type of dragon which obviously is a reference to how Gringott's bank is a reference to dragons. This led us to thinking there was obviously some dragon that we had missed and a lot of reading about dragons.

Our next guess was less reasonable. We thought it might have something to do with the Gemino and Flagrante curses put on the Lestrange Vault. The thought process was that similar to a password, the curses are also there to protect. Of course the password was 15 characters long so we tried variations of them combined. 

Our third guess required a couple jumps. First if you've played Hollow Knight, a wyrm can be a giant literal worm like creature. Is there perhaps, a giant literal worm like creature in harry potter. That's right the Basilisk. My team member who came up with this was cooking, maybe a little bit too much.
